Assignment 10: Multimedia and Images

In this assignment, we learned about multimedia and images in HTML. We learned how to add raster and vector images, audio, and video links to our web pages. We also learned how to embed YouTube videos using the iframe tag. We can apply this learning to our current or future environments by ensuring that our web pages are visually appealing and informative.

Raster and Vector Images

We added a raster image and a vector image to our web110/images folder. We then added img tags to our web110/assignment10.html file to display these images.

Audio and Video Links

We added an audio tag that links to mp3 or mp4 and oga audio files at http://media.w3.org/2010/05/sound/.

Video

We added a video tag that links to webm, ogv,and mp4 video files at http://media.w3.org/2010/05/video/.

YouTube Video

We added an iframe tag that embeds a YouTube video.
